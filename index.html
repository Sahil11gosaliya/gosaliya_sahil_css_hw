HOW TO WRITE A NOVEL :- TO DO LIST

TO DO

  • Understand what a novel is.
  • Determine the type of novelist you are.
  • Start with a novel idea.
  • Read novels in your genre.
  • Set up a writing space.
  • Research your story's idea.
  • Create a writing schedule.
  • Write the novel's premise.

IN PROGRESS

  • Decide the genre.
  • Pick a book.
  • Pick a pen.
  • Make the space clear.
  • Let the thoughts collide.
  • Start Writing!

DONE

  • Begin with introduction.
  • Introduce your characters.
  • Make the plot.
  • Reveal the twist.
  • Leave readers hooked.
  • Conclude the chapter.
